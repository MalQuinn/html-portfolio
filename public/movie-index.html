Malky's Top Flicks: Cult Classics and Hidden Gems

"Battle Britannia: A Selection of Captivating British War Films"

"Bravo Two Zero" (1998)

Bravo Two Zero depicts the story of a British SAS mission infiltrate and cut communication lines that sent messages to control the deployment of Scud missiles during the Iraq war.

"Zulu" (1964)

This movie depicts the heroic defense of a small outpost by around 150 British soldiers against a force of around 3 - 4 thousand Zulu warriors.

"Dunkirk" (2017)

Is a movie based on the true story of the evacuation of Allied soldiers from the beaches of Dunkirk during World War II.
